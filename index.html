What is SkyGuide and Why is it a Game-Changer for Flight Crews?

SkyGuide is a revolutionary contract assistant meticulously designed for flight attendants and pilots. Tired of sifting through dense contract documents? SkyGuide provides instant answers to your urgent contract questions, clarifying complex work rules, rest requirements, pay calculations, and much more – instantly and accurately.

Our intelligent system includes direct references to actual contract language, ensuring you get precise and trustworthy information right when you need it.

Initially tailored for:

  • United Airlines pilots and flight attendants
  • Alaska Airlines flight attendants
  • American Airlines pilots and flight attendants
  • Delta Airlines pilots
